God exists

Background

Background

The question of God's existence is a perennial and foundational debate spanning philosophy, religion, and arguments based upon observable facts. The central conflict lies between proponents who rely on logical arguments (cosmological, teleological, ontological), personal revelation, and faith, versus opponents who rely on scientific naturalism, empirical evidence, and logical problems like the problem of evil or divine hiddenness. The conclusion fundamentally shapes ethical systems, scientific inquiry, and views on the ultimate meaning of human life.
