Democracy is the best form of government

Background & Definitions

Background

The claim that democracy is the optimal form of political organization is intensely debated, especially amidst challenges like the rise of effective autocracies and internal democratic decay due to polarization and slow decision-making. The debate hinges on whether systems prioritizing individual liberty and responsiveness justify the inherent inefficiencies and volatility, or whether more centralized, meritocratic governance provides better long-term stability and economic prosperity.

Key Definitions

Democracy
Democracy is a contested concept covering political systems based on rule by the people; key differences exist between representative models (elected officials) and direct forms (citizen vote), as well as liberal models that prioritize individual rights and illiberal models that prioritize majoritarian will.
