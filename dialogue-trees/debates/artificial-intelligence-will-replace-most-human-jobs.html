Artificial intelligence will replace most human jobs

Background & Definitions

Background

The rapid deployment of generative AI and various forms of narrow artificial intelligence has significantly increased the capability of machines to perform complex cognitive tasks. This has intensified the debate among labor economists and technologists about whether AI will lead to widespread techno-unemployment or simply transform roles and create new industries. The core disagreement centers on the velocity and scope of automation‚Äîwhether the displacement will outpace the creation of new employment opportunities.

Key Definitions

Replace
The reduction of human contribution to an employment role to a point where the role itself is no longer economically viable for a human worker.
Job
A specific, aggregated employment role currently recognized and paid for within the economy.
Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Algorithmic systems, including large language models and specialized software, focused on performing complex cognitive or physical tasks.
AI Artificial Intelligence Technology
Proposition: Artificial intelligence will replace most human jobs
