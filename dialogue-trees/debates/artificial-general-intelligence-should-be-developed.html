Artificial general intelligence should be developed

Background & Definitions

Background

The proposition addresses whether humanity should actively pursue the creation of AGI, a hypothetical intelligence capable of performing any intellectual task a human can. The debate hinges on balancing unprecedented potential benefits, such as curing disease and solving climate change, against severe risks, including existential catastrophe, massive economic disruption, and loss of control.

Key Definitions

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)
An AI system that possesses the robust, flexible, and adaptive cognitive abilities to successfully perform any intellectual task that a human being can.
Developed
Refers to the sustained scientific, engineering, and resource commitment necessary to bring a stable, functioning, and deployable AGI prototype into existence.
AGI AI Futurism
Proposition: Artificial general intelligence should be developed
