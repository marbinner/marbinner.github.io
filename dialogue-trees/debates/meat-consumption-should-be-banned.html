Meat consumption should be banned

Background & Definitions

Background

The proposition calls for an outright prohibition of meat consumption, stemming primarily from concerns over climate change, resource depletion, and animal welfare in industrial farming. The debate pits these comprehensive environmental and ethical arguments against claims of human nutritional necessity, cultural tradition, and the massive economic impact on global food systems.

Key Definitions

Meat
The proposition refers to the edible muscle and organs derived from slaughtered vertebrates (mammals, birds, and fish) intended for human dietary intake.
Banned
A comprehensive governmental prohibition on the production, sale, distribution, and personal consumption of meat, enforced through legal penalties.
Consumption
The act of eating or utilizing meat products, implying that both the final act of ingestion and the commercial supply chain enabling it are subject to the ban.
